验签

# manjaro @ manjaro in ~/Documents/kayak [9:17:48]

解密

# manjaro @ manjaro in ~/Documents/kayak [9:29:11] 
$ sh tools/kayakTool.sh  2 私钥文件路径（推荐使用绝对路径） 私钥密码 "待解密的字符串"
{"body":"报文体","sign":"签名"}
